About Us

The Haiges family legacy in the cleaning industry spans five generations, beginning in the late 1800s with dye shops and dry-cleaners. Over time the family transitioned from laundry processing to equipment supplier and since 1967, Haiges Machinery has been a trusted leader in delivering innovative commercial and industrial laundry solutions across Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin. We specialize in providing top-quality laundry and dry cleaning equipment, tailored to meet the unique needs of industries like hospitality, healthcare, laundromats, and more. Our comprehensive services include expert equipment selection, professional installation, and reliable maintenance by certified technicians, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum efficiency for your operations.

At Haiges, we pride ourselves on being more than just a supplier—we’re a partner dedicated to your success. From new and rebuilt equipment sales to customized service plans, we offer end-to-end solutions that enhance productivity and profitability. Our commitment to exceptional customer service and industry expertise has earned us a reputation for reliability and professionalism. Whether you’re upgrading a laundromat or outfitting a large-scale industrial laundry, Haiges Machinery delivers durable, high-performance equipment backed by unparalleled support. Let us help you streamline your laundry operations with solutions designed for growth and efficiency.
